# Product Narrative

## One-line positioning

The first MCP-native AI ERP experiment.

## The provocation

You should not learn an ERP. Your ERP should understand your intent.

## What makes it different

Most AI ERP demos add a chatbot on top of old workflows. anti-ERP treats agents as the primary interface and turns backend features into explicit MCP capabilities.

The result is a product that feels closer to a business command center than a traditional menu tree.

## Portfolio angle

This project is designed to show product taste, technical architecture, AI literacy, and narrative clarity in one public prototype.
